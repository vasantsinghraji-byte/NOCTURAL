Privacy Policy

Nocturnal collects only the details needed to provide home healthcare booking, provider onboarding, and hospital pilot waitlist services in India.

Information We Collect

For patients, this may include name, mobile number, email, booking details, address, and care preferences. For providers, this may include identity, contact, registration, credential, and availability details. For hospital waitlist leads, this includes business contact details and expected staffing needs.

How We Use It

We use this information to create accounts, manage bookings, verify providers, operate the waitlist, contact you about requested services, and improve the platform.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share details only where needed to operate the service, process payments, send notifications, meet legal obligations, or protect users.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Nocturnal team through the official support channel once published.
